# CYBERTRON ACADEMY: Master Curriculum
## Aariz Nizamudin — Born ~September 2021 | Homeschool Education Plan

**Philosophy:** Interest-driven, guided-play pedagogy. Transformers lore as the engagement vehicle. Every subject taught through what he already loves. Build the engineer's mind through curiosity, not compliance.

**Frameworks Applied:**
- Vygotsky's Zone of Proximal Development (challenge without frustration)
- Dweck's Growth Mindset (praise process, not person)
- Reggio Emilia (child as protagonist, documentation, project-based)
- Montessori Sensorial/Practical Life (concrete before abstract)
- EiE Engineering Design Process (Ask, Imagine, Plan, Create, Improve)
- Florida VPK standards alignment (8 domains)

**Daily Structure (1.5-2 hours structured, rest is play):**
```
9:00-9:10   Morning Circle (calendar, weather, "Autobot roll call")

10:00-10:20 Lesson Block 2 (STEM/engineering/science)
10:20-10:40 Creative Station (coloring, drawing, building, art)
10:40-10:45 Closing Circle (what did we learn? what was hard? preview tomorrow)
```

## PHASE 2: DECODING TELETRAAN-1 (Age 4.5 - 5.0) — CURRENT
**Theme:** "Learning the Autobot Code"
**Duration:** ~6 months (April 2026 - October 2026)
**Core Focus:** Alphabet mastery, phonics foundations, counting to 30+, addition within 5, spatial vocabulary, engineering design process

### Literacy Track
- Letter recognition: all 26 uppercase, begin lowercase
- Letter-sound correspondence: consonants first, then short vowels
- Phonological awareness: rhyming, syllable clapping, initial sound isolation
- Name writing: first name fluent, last name introduced
- Sight words: 10-15 high-frequency words (the, is, a, I, my, can, see, go, to, it)
- Read-alouds: 20+ minutes daily (parent reads, child predicts/discusses)
- Narrative: sequencing 3-4 picture events, retelling simple stories

### Numeracy Track
- Counting: rote to 30+, 1:1 correspondence to 20
- Numeral writing: 0-10 fluent, 11-20 introduced
- Operations: addition within 5 with objects, "one more/one less"
- Patterns: AB, ABC, ABB — copy, extend, create
- Measurement: longer/shorter, heavier/lighter, direct comparison
- Time: morning/afternoon/night, days of the week
- Spatial vocabulary: above, below, beside, between, inside, outside, behind, in front of

### STEM/Engineering Track
- Simple machines: wheels, levers, ramps (inclined planes), gears
- Materials science: hard/soft, heavy/light, flexible/rigid, strong/weak
- Engineering design: Ask → Imagine → Plan → Create → Improve
- Building challenges: weekly escalating complexity
- Observation skills: "what do you notice?" before "what do you think?"
- Forces: push, pull, gravity, balance

### Social-Emotional Track
- Growth mindset language: "yet," process praise
- Identifying emotions: happy, sad, frustrated, proud, curious, brave
- Problem-solving: "what could you try?" before giving solutions
- Independence: dressing, breakfast prep, cleanup, choice-making
- Perseverance: normalizing struggle, celebrating iteration

### Creative/Physical Track
- Fine motor: cutting, tracing, drawing details, bead threading, clay work
- Gross motor: daily outdoor play, balance, throwing, catching
- Art: observational drawing, color mixing, free creation
- Music: rhythm, clapping patterns, simple songs
