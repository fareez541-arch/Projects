# CCRN Educational Mission Mandate

## Primary Directive
Provide comprehensive, evidence-based critical care nursing education aligned with the AACN CCRN certification blueprint to advance clinical excellence and patient outcomes in critical care environments.

## Learning Objectives

### Clinical Judgment
- Develop systematic assessment skills for hemodynamic monitoring and interpretation
- Master prioritization frameworks for multi-system organ failure scenarios
- Apply evidence-based interventions for acute cardiovascular, pulmonary, and neurological emergencies

### Technical Competency
- Demonstrate proficiency with advanced monitoring technologies (PA catheters, ICP monitoring, etc.)
- Interpret laboratory values and diagnostic studies within critical care context
- Execute complex medication calculations and titration protocols safely

### Professional Practice
- Integrate AACN Synergy Model for Patient Care™ in clinical decision-making
- Apply ethical frameworks to end-of-life and resource allocation dilemmas
- Lead interdisciplinary teams using standardized communication protocols (SBAR, closed-loop communication)

## Content Domains
1. **Cardiovascular** (20%) - Hemodynamics, shock states, advanced ECG interpretation
2. **Respiratory** (18%) - Mechanical ventilation, ARDS management, weaning protocols
3. **Neurological** (14%) - ICP management, stroke protocols, seizure disorders
4. **Renal/Genitourinary** (10%) - CRRT, electrolyte emergencies, acid-base balance
5. **Gastrointestinal** (8%) - Acute pancreatitis, GI bleeding, hepatic failure
6. **Endocrine** (8%) - DKA, HHS, adrenal crisis, thyroid storm
7. **Hematology/Immunology** (7%) - DIC, transfusion reactions, sepsis protocols
8. **Multisystem** (10%) - Sepsis, MODS, trauma, burns
9. **Behavioral/Psychosocial** (5%) - Delirium, substance withdrawal, family dynamics

## Success Metrics
- 90% first-time pass rate on CCRN certification exam
- Demonstration of clinical reasoning through case-based simulation
- Peer validation of critical thinking via structured rubric assessment
